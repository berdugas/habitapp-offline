A habit without purpose is just a task on a list.

You've described who you're becoming. Now pick an action that directly serves that identity — not just something that sounds productive.

"Read for 10 minutes" serves becoming a reader. "Drink more water" doesn't. The clearer the connection, the more natural the habit feels.

Next, you'll choose one action that brings you closer to the person you described. Ask yourself: would this person do this?
