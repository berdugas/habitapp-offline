You don't need willpower if you have a trigger.

The most reliable habits don't depend on motivation — they're attached to something you already do. Your existing routine becomes the reminder.

"After I pour my morning coffee, I'll read one page." The coffee is the cue. No alarm needed, no decision to make.

Next, you'll pick something you already do every day and attach your new habit to it. Think about the moments in your day that happen like clockwork.
